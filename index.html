School of Magic

  • Jaigo intelligence

    Jaigo is a spell system based on disruption. The form of Jaigo that the Ventril ships uses is very different from what is seen by Dresilo or human practitioners. Ventril ships use a central energy source to power huge death beams. Intelligence raises the Jaigo essence pool. If performed through an instrument, it is a very staccato, syncopated style.

  • Osu willpower

    Osu magic is deeply involved in fluid reaction, and manipulation. It can tamper with emotions, or even physics. It is much slower than Jaigo, but appears more elegant. This applies to the musical catalyst as well. Not much is known about its origins. Many assume that the Kartars developed it in a forgotten age. The Murians have also shown a great predilection for the art, using it more forcefully than Earthlings or Kartars. Your effective Osu essence pool receives a bonus from willpower. However, because of the relative slowness of Osu magic, it is possible to be interrupted.
